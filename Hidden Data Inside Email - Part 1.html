Hidden Data Inside Email - Part 1:

The Average Amazon Customer Spends $120 / month on Amazon

What if a Robot could use Email to help you save time and money?

Email is a treasure trove of insight and knowledge. At Weld, our AI, Weldbot, works with email to create structured data and find ways to help users save time, money, and hassle by proactively presenting information and solutions to everyday tasks.

We recently trained Weldbot to help you find the best prices for the products you buy online. Looking at the anonymized information – we found some interesting signals, patterns and stats in 8 months of shopping data from Amazon.com.

Fun facts during this time period:

  • The average spend per item is $33.00 / item
  • The average number of items purchased is 28.8 / user

Imagine This

What if your computer had structured information, a well organized set of knowledge, where it could identify patterns, search on your behalf, and bring you the important information and decisions. For example:

  • What if your computer could automatically identify your repeat purchases and save you money by finding you a better price? Automatically.
  • What if your computer could alert you to a product recall?
